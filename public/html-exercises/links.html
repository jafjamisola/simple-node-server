The Three Best Movies

Spirited Away


The movie is mostly seen as a coming-of-age film, where Chihiro (protagonist) enters the spirit world with little to no knowledge about it and forced to act independently. What I absolutely love about it, is the subtle entrance of the love affair between Sen and Haku (a river spirit who was always saved her). You don't even see it coming. It is because of the apparent youth of the two characters [when in fact, Haku's an old river spirit], that most viewers tend to not think that this film is a romance. That this is just a tasteful animated film about a young girl who lost her parents and would be at the beck and call of an evil lady just to get them back. But it's so much more than that.

The Harry Potter Series


I don't think this needs much justification.

Wreck-It Ralph


This film brings nostalgia and pleasurable visuals (the candy colors, guys), paired with such a relatable plot. No wonder it was such a hit, and it is still a beautiful, fun film to watch with anyone. Ralph is a character with so much to give; he is presented as a bad guy but it's hardly his personality. I find that really beautiful. In this world we live in, labels are always present; always given to people regardless of who they really are, as long as society, AKA every one else, thinks so. And that was what the film managed to accomplish. Needless to say, it is a timeless film.

The Three Best Songs

Style by Taylor Swift

I have been in love with Taylor Swift's songs since I was 12. She has always talked directly to her listeners, like she's singing what's happening in our [listeners'] lives. And this song is just one of those that are soooo nice to hear.

Worth It by Fifth Harmony

I love songs that are very enjoyable and fun to listen to. This song is it for me right now. When I hear this song, it makes me want to dance.

Trouble by Iggy Azalea feat. Jennifer Hudson

Iggy Azalea's voice is one-of-a-kind and her songs are very catchy. No wonder she skyrocketed to fame in a little time. Matched with Jennifer Hudson's skill set, this song is a very good one.
